# SmartRoad Rwanda

## Overview

SmartRoad Rwanda is a comprehensive, digital transportation infrastructure intelligence platform designed to integrate Transportation Engineering principles, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), and modern software engineering. It serves as a unified ecosystem for road asset management, traffic engineering, and infrastructure planning, providing engineers and city planners with the actionable insights necessary for sustainable network management.

## Vision

To create a modern, data-driven digital transportation management platform tailored for Rwanda and developing countries. The platform aims to replace fragmented systems with a single source of truth, enabling proactive maintenance, improved road safety, optimized mobility, and intelligent infrastructure lifecycle management.

## Core Modules

- **GIS Transportation Map:** Geospatial visualization of the entire road network.
- **Road Asset Management:** Digital inventory of pavements, bridges, and road furniture.
- **Traffic Engineering:** Analysis of traffic volumes, classifications, and network capacity.
- **Transportation Planning:** Tools for long-term infrastructure and mobility planning.
- **Road Safety:** Accident geocoding and black spot identification.
- **Public Transport:** Management and routing analytics for transit systems.
- **Analytics Dashboard:** Engineering-focused metrics and reporting.
- **AI Decision Support:** Predictive modeling for pavement degradation and maintenance optimization.

## Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** Next.js + Javascript
- **Backend:** NestJS/Node.js
- **Database:** PostgreSQL + PostGIS
- **AI:** Python
- **GIS:** Leaflet/MapLibre
